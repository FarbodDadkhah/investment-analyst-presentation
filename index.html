Investment Analyst Tool

Hello dear Primo team! due to the conspicous desire for an AI enable analyst in the position, I built an engine using python + AI to help me process information at a much more rapid pace.

  1. Recieves a main objective and sub objectvies that are stepified to complete the objective.
  2. Based on that, a Investment-analytics context engineered layer will reason and provide 20 useful links for each sub objective
  3. Then the next layer, another Investment-analytics context engineered layer will scrape information from all of these links with a confidence score and the only relevant part of the text.

As an analyst with this engine, i managed to do the analysis on a much larger amount of information but fast and make more relevant decisions. The magic that would not be possible wihtout the engine : I can just read very fast the piece of relevant text with the assigned confidence score to see if the information is relevant to what i desire, if so then and only then use the resource in the presentation.

Step 1: Define Objectives

Picking the objective + flexible sub objectives (eg. Market & Competition -> TAM/SAM/SOM for each company specifically)

Step 2: AI-Generated Resources

Useful links generated by the AI for each sub objective

Step 3: Intelligent Analysis

An analysis done to extract and provide conclusion on the relevant chunk of the scraped URL
